Document Required

For Customers :

Proof of identity : This can be a driver's license, passport, or other government-issued ID.

Proof of address : This can be a utility bill, bank statement, or other document that shows your current address.

Proof of income : This is not always required, but some website rental companies may ask for proof of income to ensure that you can afford the monthly payments.

In addition to these general documents, some website rental companies may also require specific documents, such as a business license or tax ID number. It is important to check with the specific website rental company that you are considering to see what documents they require.

Tips for submitting your documents

Make sure that all of your documents are clear and legible.

If you are submitting electronic copies of your documents, make sure that they are in a PDF or JPEG format.

If you are mailing your documents, be sure to send them certified mail with return receipt requested.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your documents

are submitted correctly and that your website

rental process is as smooth as possible.
